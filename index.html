Voiceflow Custom Actions Demo

Sample Custom Actions and utterances to trigger their flows include:

calendar

Sample utterance: Can I book an appointment?

Displays a calendar component that is imported into the demo React project.

video

Sample utterance: What services do you offer?

Displays a custom video component.

talk_to_agent

Sample utterance: Talk to an agent

Switches the conversation into a mode that emulates chatting with a live agent.

New messages will skip the Voiceflow logic and be sent directly to the agent.

You can also end the live chat and return to talking with the Voiceflow bot. (The banner is a custom component.)
